<p><p>The waste-to-energy steam turbine market includes three main types: condensing, back pressure, and others. Condensing turbines maximize energy extraction by converting steam into water, allowing for efficient electricity generation. Back pressure turbines, on the other hand, use steam at a lower pressure, providing thermal energy for industrial processes. Other types may include combined heat and power systems or steam turbines designed for specific applications. Each type caters to different operational needs and energy efficiency goals in waste management.</p></p>
