<p><p>The passenger vehicle airbag inflator market consists of three main types: </p><p>1. **Pyrotechnic Inflators** utilize a chemical reaction to rapidly produce gas, inflating the airbag in milliseconds during a collision.</p><p>   </p><p>2. **Stored Gas Inflators** rely on compressed gas stored in a cylinder, triggering a discharge to inflate the airbag.</p><p>3. **Hybrid Inflators** combine both technologies, using a pyrotechnic mechanism for initial inflation and stored gas for supplemental inflation, offering enhanced performance and safety in crash situations.</p></p>
