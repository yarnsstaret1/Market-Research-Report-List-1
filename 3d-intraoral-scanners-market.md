<p><p>The 3D intraoral scanners market comprises primarily two types: white light and laser scanners. White light scanners utilize a series of LED lights combined with cameras to capture detailed images of the oral cavity, providing high accuracy and fast scanning times. In contrast, laser scanners use focused laser beams to capture precise 3D data of dental structures, ensuring superior detail and depth perception. Both technologies advance dental diagnostics, treatment planning, and patient engagement, catering to various clinical needs and preferences.</p></p>
