<p><p>The pigment base coat market is categorized into three main types: organic, inorganic, and specialty. Organic pigments are carbon-based, offering vibrant colors and high performance, commonly used in coatings and plastics. Inorganic pigments, derived from mineral sources, are known for their durability and opacity, making them ideal for construction and automotive applications. Specialty pigments encompass unique formulations that enhance specific properties, like fluorescence or heat resistance, catering to niche markets in industries such as cosmetics and printing.</p></p>
