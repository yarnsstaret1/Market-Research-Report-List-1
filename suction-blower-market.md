<p><p>The suction blower market primarily consists of two main types: spiral fans and centrifugal fans. Spiral fans utilize a helical design that enhances airflow and efficiency, making them ideal for applications requiring high volume and low pressure. On the other hand, centrifugal fans operate by converting rotational energy into kinetic energy, effectively moving air at higher pressures. Both types cater to various industries, including HVAC, manufacturing, and automotive, each offering unique advantages in terms of performance, energy efficiency, and noise levels.</p></p>
