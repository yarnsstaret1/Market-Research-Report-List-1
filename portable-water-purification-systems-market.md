<p><p>The portable water purification systems market includes several key types: ion exchange, filtration, and distillation. Ion exchange systems remove impurities by exchanging harmful ions with less harmful ones, effectively softening water. Filtration systems utilize physical barriers, such as activated carbon or ceramic elements, to trap contaminants. Distillation involves boiling water and collecting the vapor, which leaves impurities behind, resulting in purified water. These methods cater to varied needs, providing safe drinking water in diverse situations such as outdoor activities or emergency scenarios.</p></p>
