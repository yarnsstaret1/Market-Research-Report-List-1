<p><strong>The Bucket Auger Drilling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Double Start</li><li>Single Start</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bucket auger drilling market is primarily categorized into two types: double start and single start augers. Single start augers feature a single helical flight that moves soil upward as it drills, making them ideal for less challenging terrains. In contrast, double start augers have two helical flights, allowing for faster drilling and improved soil removal efficiency, making them suitable for denser materials and larger projects. Both types cater to specific requirements based on soil conditions and project scope.</p></p>
