<p><p>The solenoid dosing pump market can be categorized into two primary types: manual control and signal control. Manual control pumps are operated by users who adjust flow rates and dosing intervals through physical settings, providing straightforward functionality for applications requiring minimal automation. In contrast, signal control pumps are integrated with automation systems, responding to external signals for precise dosing. This allows for dynamic adjustment in real-time applications, enhancing efficiency and accuracy in various industrial processes such as water treatment and chemical processing.</p></p>
